# GovIntel AI User Guide

## How to Find More Government Customers and Contracts

## What is GovIntel AI?

GovIntel AI is a Federal Sales Intelligence platform designed to help businesses answer one critical question:

**Who should I sell to next?**

GovIntel combines federal spending intelligence, competitor research, market analysis, and AI-powered business development recommendations to help companies pursue government contracts more effectively.

## Core Use Cases

### Find Government Buyers
- Identify agencies buying your products and services
- Discover spending agencies
- Prioritize targets

### Analyze Your Company
Provide a company website, capability statement, or business description and GovIntel will identify:
- Government use cases
- NAICS codes
- Likely buyers
- Competitors
- Recommended next steps

### Market Intelligence
GovIntel can analyze:
- Market size
- Competition levels
- Spending trends
- Top contractors

### Recompete Opportunities
Identify expiring contracts and potential replacement opportunities.

### Capture Strategy
Receive recommendations on agency targeting, teaming, and business development priorities.

## Recommended Prompt Templates

### Company Analysis
Analyze my company and identify federal agencies likely to buy my services. My website is: [website URL]

### Market Analysis
Analyze the federal market for [industry or service].

### Competitor Analysis
Who are the largest government contractors in [industry]?

### Agency Research
Which federal agencies spend the most money on [industry]?

### Capture Strategy
Build a federal capture strategy for my company.

## Tips for Best Results

1. Be specific about your products and services.
2. Include your website or capability statement.
3. Mention certifications such as 8(a), WOSB, SDVOSB, or HUBZone.
4. Ask follow-up questions.

## Example Workflow

1. Analyze my company.
2. Identify target agencies.
3. Analyze spending trends.
4. Identify competitors.
5. Find recompete opportunities.
6. Build a capture strategy.

## What Makes GovIntel Different?

GovIntel combines:

The goal is not simply to find contracts. The goal is to help businesses win more government customers.
